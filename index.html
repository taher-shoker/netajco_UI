Who We Are

We are a leading construction company in Saudi Arabia’s massive industry. Founded in 1974, today, we have organically grown into an entity embarking on large and complex projects securing number 12 amongst the 100 fastest growing companies in Saudi. AlFouzan specializes in general construction, including infrastructure & underground utilities, residential, healthcare facilities, and educational projects.

We have gained local recognition for our high-level professionalism; delivery Excellence; ability in rigorously adapting new technologies and standards; and for achieving superior quality to the full satisfaction of our clients. At AlFouzan, we have earned substantial experience through challenging projects as well as Expertise and insight in diverse fields of construction.
